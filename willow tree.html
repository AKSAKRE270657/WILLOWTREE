Our Company

The Mobile Innovation Agency

WillowTree helps the world’s most admired companies build applications that matter. Founded in 2008 as a mobile app and web development company, we’ve grown with the digital ecosystem to support every stage of the product life cycle.

We craft digital products that solve business needs and drive market differentiation. Our teams provide end-to-end, full-stack development (including native mobile apps, web development, voice interfaces, and cross-platform), strategy, design, and growth marketing services while baking in considerations for SEO, accessibility, and security. Our co-located teams work and play from Charlottesville, VA; Durham, NC; Columbus, OH; and New York, NY.
